Southern Fried Chicken the right way

Doesn't that just look declicious

What you will need?

  • pepper
  • buttermilk
  • bread crumbs
  • sea salt
  • vegetable oil
  • 1kg chicken pieces
  • Flour
  • eggs
  • lemon
  • paprika
  • chilli (optional)

How to make it?

  1. Part 1 -Preparation

    1. Clean the chicken pieces of any feathers
    2. Wash the chicken with cold water
    3. Cover the chicken with 3 tbsp of sea salt
    4. Massage it all over the chicken
    5. Then squeeze half a lemon onto the chicken pieces
    6. Place the chicken into the fridge and leave it for as long as you want or skip this step
    7. Take the chicken out of the fridge and then pour buttermilk all over the chicken pieces
    8. Put pack in the fridge for 2 hours minimum

  2. Part 2- Making

    1. Measure 2 cups of flour and place it into a bowl
    2. Whisk 3 eggs and place it into a seperate bowl
    3. Add 2 tbsp of paprika to the flour
    4. Add any other seasonings you want as well to the flour
    5. Add 3 cups of breadcrumbs to a seperate bowl
    6. Pick up a chicken piece and dip it into the whisked egg
    7. Place the chicken into the flour
    8. Place the chicken into the breadcrumbs
    9. Put the chicken to the side and repeat for all the chicken pieces or move straight to part 3
    10. Make sure the chicken is covered in the breadcrumbs if not repeat step vi

  3. Part 3- Cooking

    1. Heat the oil
    2. When it is ready place the chicken into the oil and fry for 4/5 minutes until the outside is crispy and inside is cooked
    3. Put a few sprinkles of sea salt and pepper onto the chicken pieces and serve it with any side you prefer

      4. We generally recommend sour cream and chive dip along with curly fries Click to watch tutorial on how to make a dip
